# GP-15 — Access Review & Offboarding Procedure

## Trigger
Scheduled access review window veya actor departure / role removal.

## Roles
- access owner
- people or operations coordinator
- system owner

## Steps
1. Target actor listesi ve privilege scope'u çıkar.
2. Active grants, temporary accesses ve expiries'i gözden geçir.
3. Artık gerekmeyen access'leri remove planına bağla.
4. Offboarding completion evidence kaydet.
5. Exceptions varsa owner ve expiry ile işaretle.

## Completion
- review_completed
- offboarding_completed
- followup_required
