# GP-16 — Compliance Evidence Collection Procedure

## Trigger
Scheduled audit window, certification prep veya regulatory review.

## Roles
- compliance owner
- evidence coordinators
- control owners

## Steps
1. Required control and scope listesi oluştur.
2. Evidence sources ve owners eşlemesini yap.
3. Missing evidence alanlarını işaretle.
4. Evidence bundle refs topla ve quality check yap.
5. Review summary ve remaining gaps kaydı üret.

## Completion
- evidence_bundle_completed
- evidence_bundle_partial
- evidence_missing_blocker
